# Unit Economics

## Lead Value to Credex
A converted lead (a company that moves beyond the free audit to a full Credex engagement) is worth approximately **$12,000 ARR** (base) plus **$5,000–$50,000 in one-time success fees** (typically 20% of the first year's realized savings).
- **Assumed LTV**: $17,000 (Year 1).

## CAC by Channel
- **Organic Social (X/HN)**: $0 (Time-intensive).
- **VC Distribution**: $0 (Relationship-driven).
- **Paid (Future)**: Estimating $15 per "Audit Completed" via LinkedIn Ads (targeting Ops/Eng leads). At a 10% booking rate, **CAC per consultation is $150**.

## Profitability Math
To be profitable, we need to balance the consultation effort against the LTV:
- **Conversion funnel**:
    - Audit Completed: 1,000
    - Consult Booked (10%): 100
    - Credit Purchase / Full Engagement (10% of consults): 10
- **Revenue**: 10 x $17,000 = **$170,000**
- **Operating Costs**:
    - Anthropic/Resend/Supabase: ~$500/mo ($6,000/yr)
    - Sales/Consultant Time: $500 per consult x 100 = $50,000
- **Net Profit**: $170,000 - $56,000 = **$114,000 per 1,000 audits**.

## Path to $1M ARR (18 Months)
1. **Automated Audits**: Reach 10,000 audits per month through viral loops and VC partnerships.
2. **Consultation Tiering**: Automate the "Low Value" (<$500/mo savings) consultations with an AI-agent bot, focusing human consultants only on "High Value" (> $2k/mo savings) cases.
3. **Credit Reselling**: Partner with OpenAI/Anthropic to become a certified credit reseller, taking a 3-5% margin on the spend we optimize.
4. **Retention**: Transition from a one-time audit to a "Continuous Monitoring" SaaS product ($99/mo) that alerts companies when they have zombie seats.
   - 1,000 companies @ $99/mo = $1.2M ARR.
