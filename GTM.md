# Go-To-Market (GTM) Strategy

## Target User
The exact target user is a **Founding Engineer** or **Operations Lead** at a series-A B2B SaaS startup (team size 15–40). This person is technical enough to choose the tools but has recently been tasked by the CEO/CFO to "cut the fat" from the monthly burn. They feel personally responsible for the stack but are too busy to manually cross-reference 8 different pricing pages.

## Search & Scroll Context
Right before wanting this tool, they are:
- Searching: "Cursor vs GitHub Copilot Business features"
- Searching: "Claude Team plan minimum seats"
- Scrolling: Hacker News threads about "AI tool fatigue" or "SaaS spend management."
- Looking at their Stripe dashboard or Ramp/Mercury spend report and wondering why the "AI tools" category is growing 20% MoM.

## Online Hangouts
- **Subreddits**: r/startups, r/saas, r/experienceddevs.
- **Slack/Discord**: Elixir Slack (high density of elite/solopreneurs), Ben's Bites Discord, various "Y Combinator" alumni lists.
- **X (Twitter)**: Following accounts like @swyx, @levelsio, and @gregisenberg.

## The First 100 Users ($0 Budget)
1. **The "Check My Stack" Thread (Day 1-5)**: Post a screenshot of a high-savings audit on X with the tag #buildinpublic. Tag tool founders (e.g., Cursor) not to bash them, but to ask for clarification on "optimal usage." This usually triggers a thread where people reply with their own stacks for an "instant audit."
2. **Reddit Utility Posting (Day 6-15)**: Find 5+ threads in r/startups asking about AI tool choices. Instead of pitching, provide a 3-sentence summary of the price differences and link to the tool as a "free calculator I built to solve this."
3. **Founder DM Campaign (Day 16-30)**: Cold DM 50 founders of recently funded companies (via Crunchbase/HN Who is Hiring). Messaging: "Hey [Name], congrats on the round. I built a tool that saved my last team $4k/yr on AI spend in 2 mins. Free to use if you want to run your current stack through it."

## Unfair Distribution Channel
Credex’s existing relationships with **Venture Capital (VC) platform teams**. Credex can offer this tool as a "Portfolio Health Check" to VCs. The VC sends a single link to 50 portfolio companies; companies save money (extending their runway), and Credex captures 50 high-intent leads for deep optimization consulting in one click.

## Week 1 Traction Goal
- **1,000 Unique Visitors**: Driven primarily by a single successful X thread or HN post.
- **200 Audits Completed**: (20% conversion).
- **40 Leads Captured**: (20% lead gate conversion).
- **5 Credex Consultations Booked**: (12.5% of leads).
- **Result**: Immediate proof of value and $0 CAC.
